Who is John Godwin and What is His Net Worth?

John Godwin is a television personality and a member of the cast of the A&E show “Duck Dynasty.” He has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million dollars.

Godwin grew up hunting deer with his father, but his fascination with duck calls began when he placed third in his first calling contest as a teenager. He has worked for Duck Commander since 2002, where he is responsible for building duck calls, managing supplies, and overseeing the shipping department.

During duck season, Godwin’s main duty is decoy technician. He determines the exact spots the decoys should be placed and is responsible for all of the equipment after the hunt to make sure it is ready for the next day’s hunt.

“Duck Dynasty” follows the Robertson family of Louisiana, who struck it rich when they began making duck calls for hunting. Godwin is one of the few members of the cast who is not part of the Robertson family. The company is run by two generations of Robertsons and their families, as well as a variety of employees. Before joining Duck Commander, Godwin worked at the Riverwood International Paper Mill for more than 20 years.
