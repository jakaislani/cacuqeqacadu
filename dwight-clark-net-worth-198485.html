Dwight Clark Net Worth

Dwight Clark was born on January 8, 1957 in Kinston, NC. He was a former wide receiver for the San Francisco 49ers and is best known for making the iconic "Catch" in the 1982 NFC Championship Game against the Dallas Cowboys. This catch is widely considered to be one of the most memorable plays in NFL history and cemented Clark's legacy as a 49ers legend.
