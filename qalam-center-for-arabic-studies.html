Why Choose Qalam wa Lawh?

Qalam wa Lawh Center for Arabic Studies is renowned for its excellent teaching of Arabic as a foreign language and for its study abroad programs. Located in Rabat, Morocco, the international language school attracts students from around the world who wish to enhance their Arabic language skills. Qalam wa Lawh offers students an opportunity to learn Arabic quickly and with greater retention through immersion in the language and cultural activities. The institution provides a variety of study abroad programs tailored to meet individual needs and schedules, including Intensive Arabic Courses, Area Studies courses, Volunteer Opportunities, Cultural Enrichment activities, and homestays. With year-round start dates and flexible course lengths, there are no obstacles preventing you from pursuing your dream of studying Arabic abroad at Qalam wa Lawh. Qalam wa Lawh's campus is situated in the heart of Rabat, within walking distance of historic sites, local shops, restaurants, and public transportation. The campus features large air-conditioned classrooms, conference rooms, computer labs, and a restaurant, making it the perfect destination for international students.
