Who won The Masked Singer season 6?

The Masked Singer show is a guessing game for a singing competition based on the Korean format King of Mask Singer.

On this show, 12 celebrity performers disguise themselves in costumes.

Each week, one singer is eliminated and unmasked.

Small hints are provided to help the viewers guess along making it extremely entertaining.

Who were the top three finalists of The Masked Singer season 6

The top 3 finalists of this seasons show were Jewel, Todrick Hall and Banana Split(Duo).

Who won The Masked Singer season 6?

Jewel was revealed as the Season 6 winner of “The Masked Singer,” having dazzled the show’s panelists and fans while disguised as the Queen of Hearts.

Jewel defeated runner-up Todrick Hall, who was later revealed to be the Bull.

Banana Split revealed to be Katharine McPhee and David Foster, were certainly intriguing, as they performed Pink’s “A Million Dreams” and Michael Bublé’s “Cry Me a River.” on the epic night.
