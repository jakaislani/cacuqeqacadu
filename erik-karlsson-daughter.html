Erik Karlsson Daughter: Meet Harlow Rain Karlsson

Erik Karlsson Daughter: Meet Harlow Rain Karlsson: Erik Karlsson, officially known as Erik Sven Gunnar Karlsson is a Swedish professional ice hockey player.

He developed a passion for playing ice hockey at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after ice hockey players.

At this time of writing (Monday, 7th August 2023), Karlsson is a professional ice hockey defenceman for the Pittsburgh Penguins of the National Hockey League.

Karlsson made his Swedish Hockey League debut (Elitserien) for Frolunda HC on 1st March 2008, recording 8:48 of ice time.

He was drafted in the first round, 15th overall, by the Ottawa Senators at the 2008 NHL Entry Draft.

Prior to the 2008 NHL Entry Draft, he was ranked fourth among European skaters by the NHL’s Central Scouting Service.

Karlsson spent his first nine NHL seasons with the Ottawa Senators and has also played for the San Jose Sharks.

On 13 September 2018, he was traded to the San Jose Sharks. He scored his first goal with the Sharks on 18 November in a 4–0 win over the St. Louis Blues.

On 17 June 2019, Karlsson signed a new eight-year, $92 million contract to remain with the Sharks, with an annual value of $11.5 million

The new contract made Karlsson the highest-paid defenceman in league history and the third-highest-paid player in the league at the time

During the 2022–23 NHL season Karlsson scored his first NHL career hat-trick on 1 November 2022 in a 6–5 shootout loss to the Anaheim Ducks

On 10th April 2023, Karlsson became the first defenceman to score 100 points in a season. He went ahead to finish the season with 101 points.

He was named to the NHL First All-Star team on 26 June 2023. That same year (2023), Karlsson was awarded the Victoria Award.

Karlsson is a three-time winner of the James Norris Memorial Trophy as the NHL’s best defenceman, winning the award in 2012, 2015, and 2023.

On Saturday, 6th August 2023, Karlsson was traded to the Pittsburgh Penguins in a three-team deal also involving the Montreal Canadiens.

Karlsson plays for the Swedish National Men’s Ice Hockey Team. He was selected as the tournament’s best defenceman at the 2008 IIHF World U18 Championships

On 1 March 2016, he was announced to represent Sweden at the 2016 World Cup of Hockey. In September, Karlsson was named alternate captain alongside Daniel Sedin.

Erik Karlsson Daughter: Meet Harlow Rain Karlsson

Erik Karlsson is blessed with two children; a daughter, Harlow Rain Karlsson, born on October 3, 2019, and a son, Stellan Axel Karlsson, born on February 2, 2022.

The Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman shares both children with his second wife and current spouse Melinda Currey.

On 22 November 2017, Karlsson and Malinda announced via Instagram that they were expecting their first child and on 18 December, they revealed it was a boy. The couple lost the baby just a month before he was supposed to be born.
