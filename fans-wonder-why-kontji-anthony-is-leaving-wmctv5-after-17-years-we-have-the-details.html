Fans Wonder Why Kontji Anthony Is Leaving WMCTV5 After 17 Years, We Have The Details

Broadcast journalist Kontij Anthony has left the anchor desk at WMCTV5, Action News 5, Memphis, after thinking of reevaluating what she has to do during the COVID pandemic. 

She joined the channel in 2005, and her contract expired recently on April 15. Let us have a glance at her leaving the show. 

Why Is Kontji Anthony Leaving WMCTV5?

Kontij Anthony announced in a letter on March 7 about her leaving the position after the contract expires in April. She is a former evening co-anchor at the show.

Her seventeen years of experience serving the Mid-South community has collected immense memorable moments, and she has said to cherish the days forever.

Her professional career was inaugurated at seventeen as a host for a high school television station. The same year she started working for TV stations in New York, Missouri, California, and North Carolina and finally made her path to Memphis and WMC.

Where Is Kontij Anthony Going?

Anthony has been enriched by the Action News 5. At the time of the announcement, she said, “it is time for me to take a new direction.”

Though she has left WMCTV5, she will continue anchoring the 5, 6, and 10 p.m. newscasts through mid-April.

Probably, she is giving some quality time with her husband Patrick Hendricks after the break from the show. On July 4, 2015, the couple tied knots in Memphis, Tennessee. There is no report of the couple being procreators till now.

Anthony graduated from Howard University School of Communications. Her knowledge in journalism has made her stand out in her profession.

Kontij has also reported at KTVO in Kirksville, KBAK in Bakersfield, Ca., KOVR-KMAX in Sacramento, and WNCT in Greenville, N.C.

Aside from being a journalist and anchor, she is an active member of several organizations, including the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, the National Association of Black Journalists & the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.
