Christian Bales rumored DC return has fans throwing the curve ball to end them all

The DCU rumor mill is currently abuzz with speculation that The Flash won’t just feature Michael Keaton and Ben Affleck’s Batmen, but one more previous cinematic portrayal will turn up that’ll go on to appear in Batman: The Brave and the Bold. If true, there’s only one plausible candidate. With Val Kilmer sadly recovering from a brutal battle with throat cancer and George Clooney considering Batman and Robin one of the worst choices of his career, that leaves the Dark Knight Trilogy’s Christian Bale.
