The Founders of Sand Cloud

Before diving into the net worth, it’s important to understand the brains behind Sand Cloud. The company was founded by three friends, Bruno Aschidamini, Steven Ford, and Brandon Leibel, who shared a passion for the ocean and a vision for a sustainable business model. Their personal commitment to ocean conservation has been a driving force behind the brand’s ethos and marketing strategy.

Sand Cloud’s Shark Tank Journey

Sand Cloud’s appearance on “Shark Tank” was a pivotal moment for the company. The founders pitched their business to the Sharks in 2017, seeking an investment to help grow their brand. They successfully secured a deal with Robert Herjavec, who offered $200,000 for a 15% stake in the company. This partnership provided the capital and expertise needed to scale their operations.

Post-Shark Tank Growth

After their successful pitch, Sand Cloud’s growth trajectory skyrocketed. The company expanded its product line beyond beach towels to include water bottles, apparel, and accessories.
