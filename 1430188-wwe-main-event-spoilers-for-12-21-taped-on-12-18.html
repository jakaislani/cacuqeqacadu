WWE Main Event Spoilers For 12/21 (Taped On 12/18)

The spoilers (courtesy of Anthony Mango of ewrestlingnews.com) are as follows:

  • Indi Hartwell def. Elektra Lopez
  • Apollo Crews def. Brooks Jensen
